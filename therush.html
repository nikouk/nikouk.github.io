THE RUSH

An ongoing collective of photos from peak travel times in London; a look into the fast-paced lives of morning/evening commuters.

This is a currently ongoing project to document and explore the vibrant community of London’s commuters and explore the struggles of the rush to and from work. This project started with a college shoot that took me into Waterloo at 8:00am and, since then, I have been keeping up documenting these interesting people’s lives.
