AMITY

A close, intimate look at friends and family and the relationship between photographer and subject. All shot on/edited in the style of 35mm film.

This project took place over quite a few months and ended with the development of 3 rolls of old film that I remembered were just sat around undeveloped. Getting them developed allowed me to look back at the time that had gone by and the nostalgia this gave me inspired me to shoot other things in the same style; with a 35mm film ‘look’.
