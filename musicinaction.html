MUSIC IN ACTION

Samples of my work as a music photographer from theatres and small venues alike.

Samples of my work shooting gigs. I had to be adaptable and work in different environments, with the right gear for the job each time. All of these photos have different qualities to them as the bands required different styles of photography and editing but they’re all vibrant and energetic photographs that illustrate musical performance.
